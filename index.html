Who We Are

At Little Stars Babysitting, we’re built on years of genuine childcare experience. Our founder spent four wonderful years as a registered childminder, caring for little ones in a nurturing, safe, and homely environment. Although she took a short break from childminding, her passion for working with children never faded. That love for childcare and helping families is what inspired the idea for Little Stars. We understand how important it is to find someone you can truly trust with your child, and we’re here to offer care that feels like family.

Why Choose Little Stars?

  • ✔️ DBS Checked and First Aid Certified
  • ✔️ Flexible hours, including evenings and weekends
  • ✔️ Activities that engage and educate

Our Services

Full-Day Childminding

Structured, reliable daytime care in a warm home setting. Ideal for working parents, with hours from 8am to 6pm.

Wraparound Care

Before and after school support including drop-offs, pick-ups, meals, and fun activities in a safe environment.

Occasional & Flexible Sessions

Perfect for parents who need occasional or last-minute care, including part-day sessions or ad hoc availability.
